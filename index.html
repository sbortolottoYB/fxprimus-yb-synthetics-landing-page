FAQ

Quick answers for brokers.

My clients deposit small — will this work?

Small deposits are the use case. The commercial logic of synthetic instruments is specifically designed for $100-$500 accounts — the exact profile that dominates emerging markets and that standard CFDs fail to monetise efficiently. High effective leverage on Tier 1 instruments means a $200 account accesses real trading exposure.

Will this cannibalise my FX volume?

No. Synthetic indices are an additional product, not a replacement for FX. Synthetics also run when FX is closed or thin — weekends, public holidays. Traders on FX during market hours do not switch to synthetics instead; they use synthetics when nothing else is available. The volatility has proven to be very attractive.
